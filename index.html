About Me

I’m Soule — an R&B singer-songwriter who turns real life into music. From heartbreak to homelessness, from divorce to rebuilding myself, every song I write comes from a place I’ve actually lived.

My mission is simple: create music with truth, soul, and emotional depth — and share it with artists who want something real.
