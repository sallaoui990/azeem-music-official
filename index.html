About Me

I’m Azeem — an R&B singer-songwriter who turns real life into music. From heartbreak to homelessness, from separation to rebuilding myself, every song comes from something I’ve lived.

My style blends the soul of 90s/2000s R&B with the vulnerability of today’s storytelling — smooth, emotional, grounded. Real pain… real healing… real love.

What I Create

  • R&B and pop songs with deep emotional truth
  • Hooks & melodies born from lived experience
  • Music that hits the heart before it hits the charts
  • Songs artists can embody, not just perform
