About Azeem

I’m Azeem — an R&B singer-songwriter who writes from real life. My music is born from the kind of experiences that leave marks: heartbreak, divorce, homelessness, fatherhood, and spiritual rebirth. I don’t write for trends. I write from truth.

My Story

Years ago, I went homeless in Paris. No safety net, no family support — just faith, fire, and music. Life broke me open, but it also gave me depth and emotional honesty. Every lyric I write carries a piece of what I lived.

I’ve rebuilt myself many times. Every comeback became a song. Every wound became a melody.

My Sound

My style blends the soul of 90s/2000s R&B with the vulnerability of today’s storytelling. Smooth, emotional, grounded — never artificial. Real pain… real healing… real love.
